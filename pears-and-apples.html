Pears, Insanely Good

Published: Tue 03 October 2023
By T. Philus Bulgaricus

In My Yogurt Journey.

My neighbor gave me this amazing pear straight off the their tree. Both pears and apples with subtle and sweet flavor, are paired well with the tart greek yogurt taste.

Both pears and apples are excellent sources of vitamins and minerals. I prefer smaller and less water rich fruits. If I'm stuck with a very watery and less flavorful apple, I add a handfull of berries if in season. If they are not in season, I will usually thaw a handful of frozen mango.
