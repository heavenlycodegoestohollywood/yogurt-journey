Strawberries are a Kind of Rose

Published: Sun 01 October 2023
By T. Philus Bulgaricus

In My Yogurt Journey.

During the Summer months when I have strawberries in the garden I like adding cut strawberries to plain yogurt. I like to serve them in glasses so you can see fruit even when it is covered in yogurt.

Biologically speaking, strawberries belong to the Rosaceae family and the subfamily Rosoideae, which also includes roses.
