Eat a Banana Today

Published: Mon 02 October 2023
By T. Philus Bulgaricus

In My Yogurt Journey.

Fortunately bananas are always in season somewhere and sent across the globe to make a great addition to any yogurt serving -- breakfast, lunch and dinner. A banana elevates your humble yogurt to something else.

With one banana you add fiber for better digestion, vitamin C for your immune, vitamin B6 for improved metabolism, potassium and magnesium for improved blood pressure, blood sugar and more.
